Our Story

More Than Just Window Cleaners

Local hustle, squeegees, and surprisingly good manners.

Who We Are

We’re Hydro Heroes — your local cleaning crew armed with squeegees, hustle, and a passion for clarity. We’re not your average window cleaners. We’re high school entrepreneurs from the Upper Valley who decided to skip the typical summer job and build something of our own.

Armed with some basic tools, YouTube tutorials, and way too much time in Google Sheets, we launched Hydro Heroes to bring top-tier service to homes and businesses in our community.

How It Started

We started out simply: walking or biking to our early jobs (back when a driver’s license was still just a dream), knocking on doors, and doing everything ourselves — from scheduling and quoting to scrubbing and rinsing.

It was equal parts chaos and learning curve, but every clean window pushed us to keep going and get better.

How It’s Going

These days, we’re a well-oiled machine. We’ve upgraded our gear, expanded our schedule, and polished our process to perfection.

But we’ve kept the things that matter most: attention to detail, respect for your space, and the kind of customer service you’d expect from a business twice our age.

Why People Choose Us

  • We show up on time (yes, really — teenagers with alarm clocks!)
  • We treat your property like it’s our own with utmost care and respect.
  • We care about quality — no streaks, no shortcuts, guaranteed.
  • We’re easy to work with — friendly, responsive, and just a text away.

Our Mission

"To bring light, clarity, and a little sparkle to the homes and businesses in our community — one window at a time. And maybe change a few minds about what young people are capable of along the way."
